INTRODUCTION

Hello, welcome to my homepage. My name is Wangleliu Chen. I come from China, and I am a student of Interactive Media in University College Cork. The MSc in Interactive Media is an intensive taught course focusing on the practical and technical aspects of interactive media. The broad aim of the course is to equip students from a wide range of backgrounds with a thorough understanding of the technology and industry-standard tools used in the digital media sector.

My interest to MSc Interactive Media was triggered by my experience on front-end design when I was an undergraduate student. In a university innovative program, my task was to design and develop the front-end part of a network security contest platform. That was my first time to use HTML. Associated with JavaScript, jQuery and Ajax, I went through challenges and complete the program with my team. Particularly, we solved the problem of logging in with Ajax, which gave me a great sense of achivement. However, since I haven't work in IT for years after graduation, I lost most of these knowledge and skills. I hope that after finishing the study of this project, I can retrive my menmory and independently create websites with both functional and aesthetic features.
